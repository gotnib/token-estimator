How it works
Your daily workflow with TokenLens
01
Optimize as you type
The extension shows your live token count inside Claude or ChatGPT. One click trims the waste — filler, hedging, redundant context — without losing meaning.
02
Save your best sessions
When a chat produces something worth keeping, click “Save Chat as Blueprint.” The full conversation is captured and sent to your TokenLens library — raw, no analysis yet.
03
Rebuild with variable slots
In the library, click “Create Blueprint.” Claude extracts the workflow, removes the noise, and wraps every project-specific value in [VARIABLE_NAME] slots. Fill them in, export plain text, paste into your next chat.
