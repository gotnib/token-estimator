Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 27, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it. We do not sell your data. We do not use your prompts to train AI models.

1. Who we are

TokenLens is operated at tokenlens.live. We provide a prompt analysis tool powered by Anthropic's Claude API. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at support@tokenlens.live.

2. What data we collect

Data Why we collect it How long we keep it
Email address To create and identify your account Until account deletion
Password (hashed) To authenticate your account Until account deletion
Usage counts To enforce plan limits and reset windows Until account deletion
Plan and subscription status To determine what features you can access Until account deletion
Stripe customer ID To manage your billing and subscription Until account deletion
Prompts (Pro plan only) To power the prompt history and library feature Until you delete them or close your account
IP address and browser info Standard server logs for security and debugging 30 days

Free and Plus plan users' prompts are sent to Anthropic's API for analysis and are not stored by TokenLens after the result is returned. Pro users' prompts are stored only to power the Library feature and can be deleted at any time.

3. How we use your data

We use the data we collect solely to:

  • Provide and improve the TokenLens service
  • Authenticate your account and enforce plan limits
  • Process payments and manage your subscription via Stripe
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmation, billing receipts)
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Respond to support requests

We do not use your prompts to train AI models. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-party services

TokenLens relies on the following third-party services to operate:

  • Anthropic — Your prompts are transmitted to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis. Anthropic processes prompts in accordance with their own Privacy Policy. We do not send any personally identifiable information alongside your prompts.
  • Supabase — We use Supabase to store account data and prompt history. Data is stored on servers in the United States. Supabase's privacy policy applies to data stored on their infrastructure.
  • Stripe — We use Stripe to process payments. We never see or store your full card details. Stripe's processing is governed by their own Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel — Our application is hosted on Vercel. Standard server logs including IP addresses may be retained by Vercel per their data retention policies.

5. Cookies and tracking

TokenLens uses minimal cookies strictly necessary to operate the service:

  • Authentication cookies — set by Supabase to keep you logged in across sessions
  • Session cookies — temporary cookies cleared when you close your browser

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics that follow you across the web. We do not display ads.

6. Data security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Database access is protected by row-level security policies
  • API keys and secrets are stored as encrypted environment variables
  • We never store payment card details — all payments are handled by Stripe

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to how we process your data
  • Withdrawal — withdraw consent at any time by closing your account

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@tokenlens.live. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you deactivate your account, your data is retained in a deactivated state. If you request full deletion, we will permanently delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or financial compliance purposes (such as billing records, which may be retained for up to 7 years).

Pro plan prompt history is retained until you delete individual items or close your account.

9. Children's privacy

TokenLens is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us at support@tokenlens.live and we will promptly delete the account.

10. International users

TokenLens is operated from the United States. If you access the service from outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country. By using TokenLens, you consent to this transfer.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or California, additional rights may apply to you under GDPR, UK GDPR, or the CCPA respectively. Contact us to exercise these rights.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by displaying a notice in the app. The date at the top of this page reflects when the policy was last updated. Continued use of TokenLens after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
